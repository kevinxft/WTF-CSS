必填项和聚焦背景变色

首字母大写和改变选中样式

The beauty of nature is always awe-inspiring. As I sit here in the midst of rolling hills and lush greenery, I am filled with a sense of peace and tranquility. The twittering of birds, the rustling of leaves, and the gentle breeze blowing across my face all combine to create a perfect moment of serenity. In this moment, I am reminded of the smallness of my problems and the greatness of the world around me. The natural world has a way of putting things into perspective, reminding us to appreciate the simple things and find joy in the present moment. As I close my eyes and take a deep breath, I am grateful for this moment of stillness and the beauty of the world that surrounds me.
